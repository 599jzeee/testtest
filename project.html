Project

This is a consultancy project for a company that produced lighting. Their demand is to cooperate with other enterprised and to utilize the lighting with interactive products.

The final concept focuses on the hearing impaired people and is dedicated to improving thier lives. The light is used in various scenarios with an APP to tell the people what happen. Certain situations can also be comstomized by users. I joined all the research and ideation process and deliver the UI design.
